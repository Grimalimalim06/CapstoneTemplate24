Overview

Background

I did research on how students in K-12 schools, particularly grades 6-12, have been scoring. What was shown that since the pandemic, students have faced significant struggles trying to pay attention and learn material in class.

Danger of Sleep Deprivation

Although this is largely self-explanatory, performance in school plays an instrumental role in ones financial success in life. Students who spend their youngers years constantly distracted, often for reasons they can't control, are put at a disadvantage compared to students who can easily stay on task. This app will hopefully be able to assist students who struggle with staying on task.

About Me

My name is Henry Goodson. I love music, soccer, skiing, and football. I'm a huge 49er fan and a huge Arsenal fan. I personally have struggled greatly with staying on task over my years in k-12 schools, which was the main reason I decided to make my app based on helping students with focus.
